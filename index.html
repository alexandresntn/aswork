About

Alexandre Santana & Co

"The challenge is to deeply understand the core of each business and apply those findings to an authentic, functional brand identity. From there, the work expands across every layer of the brand: digital products and websites built end to end, UX/UI design focused on clarity and conversion, and the technical and production design that makes everything work in the real world, from artwork finalization to print and packaging.

The focus of every project is to align the company's commercial strategy with its brand strategy to reach its goals. Creating experiences of value for people and competitive advantages in the market."
